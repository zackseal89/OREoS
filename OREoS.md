# OREoS
> The AI Marketing Operating System

## Vision

OREoS is an AI-powered marketing operating system that helps businesses transform their products into complete, high-performing marketing campaigns.

Unlike traditional social media schedulers or AI writing tools, OREoS manages the entire content lifecycle—from discovering a business's brand identity to creating, scheduling, publishing, monitoring, and continuously improving marketing content.

The platform is designed to work even without AI. AI enhances existing workflows rather than replacing them.

---

# Core Philosophy

A business should only need to tell OREoS one thing:

> "This is my business."

From there, OREoS understands the brand and becomes the company's marketing workspace.
